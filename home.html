How to play:

This device will create a series of tones and lights. You need to repeat the sequence. If you succeeds, the series becomes progressively longer and more complex. Once you fails:

  • in strict mode: the game is over.
  • in non-strict mode: you will lose one life and game is over when no life left (You have 5 lives in total).

Try it! Have Fun!
